Start game with:
	start_game.

Select piece with:
	XY.
Where X is a letter from 'a' to 'i'
and Y is a number from '1' to '9'.
Example:
a5.

Define movement with:
	OD.
Where O is either the 'l' (left, or counter-clockwise), 'f' (forward), or 'r' (right, or clockwise) letter
and D is a number from '1' to '8', limited by your 'movement power' (see rules)
and position in the board.
Example:
f1.

Define orientation (when a change of sector occurs) with:
	O.
Where O, like previously, is either the 'l', 'f', or 'r' letter.
Example:
r.
